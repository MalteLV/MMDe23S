Character Description:

The First Doctor is the protagonist of the series "Doctor Who" and the first incarnation of the Time Lord. He is an older, wise man with a distinctive character. In his first incarnation, he is often stern and authoritarian but also kind-hearted and curious. The Doctor travels in a time machine called the TARDIS, which looks like a British police call box on the outside. Despite his elderly appearance, he is full of energy and a thirst for adventure.

Companions

During his adventures in the 1960s, the First Doctor travels with various companions, including his granddaughter Susan Foreman and others such as Ian Chesterton and Barbara Wright. The dynamic interpersonal relationship between the Doctor and his companions is a crucial part of the story.

Attire

The First Doctor often wears a long coat, a vest, a bowtie, and a hat, giving him a classic Victorian or Edwardian appearance.
