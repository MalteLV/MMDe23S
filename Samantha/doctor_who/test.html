Character Description:

The Second Doctor is the second incarnation of the Time Lord and is vastly different from his predecessor. He is more playful, eccentric, and humorous. His attire is often more colorful and less formal, reflecting his cheerful character. Despite his whimsical nature, he remains highly intelligent and continues to fight the universe's adversaries.

Companions

During his adventures in the 1960s, the Second Doctor travels with various companions, including Jamie McCrimmon and Zoe Heriot. His close bond with his companions and his thirst for adventure remain central themes of the series.

Attire

The Second Doctor often wears a dark suit, a shirt with a bowtie, and a hat. His appearance is striking and sets him apart from his predecessor.
