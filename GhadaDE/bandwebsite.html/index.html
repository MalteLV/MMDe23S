NEW MUSIC WINTER 2023

We are The Amplifetes a 4-piece band from Sweden. We make music since 2008 and released our first album in 2012. Our spectrum, include 60s psychedelia, the Electric Light Orchestra, The Ramones, Elvis Costello, David Bowie, and Chicago Trax Records. Each of us have had success as songwriters and producer, we worked with a lot of different artists, for example Kelis, Britney Spears, Madonna, Kylie Minogue, and Grandmaster Flash.
